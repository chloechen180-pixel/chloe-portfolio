About Me

A passionate designer who loves telling stories through colors and shapes

Imaginative Designer

Hi! I'm Chloe, a designer passionate about creating visual magic. I believe good design can inject soul and charm into ordinary things.

My design style blends cute elements with modern aesthetics, especially loving to use bright colors and rounded shapes to create delightful visual experiences. Whether it's food packaging, brand identity, or product design, I'm committed to creating solutions that are both beautiful and practical.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy trying new baking recipes, collecting cute little objects, and drawing portraits of my cat!
